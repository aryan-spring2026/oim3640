## My Project Proposal

## What I'm building:
[For my first mini project, I want to build a tool that assists users with estimating and comparing international flight costs with respect to the following factors: duration of travel, month of travel, and choice of destination.]

## Why I chose this:
[As someone who enjoys international travel and emerging in a wide variety of different cultures, I have found it difficult to travel consistently due to improper planning and high prices. Therefore, I wanted to build a tool that enables avid travelers (such as myself) to travel more frequently with less discrepencies.]

## Core features:
## 1
Enables the user to input their departing airport, arrival airport, country they intend to visit, duration of the trip, and which month of the year they plan to travel.

##2
Showcase an estimate from a pricing perspective based on the magnitude of travelers and choice of destination.

##3
Comparison between up to 3 travel iteinaries from a cumulative perspective.

##4
Display a clean summary of trip details at the end.

## What I don't know yet:
##1:
Structuring the logical reasoning behind pricing estimations without using an API.

##2:
How will I enforce the comparsion tool when users are looking at a wide variety of travel destinations?
