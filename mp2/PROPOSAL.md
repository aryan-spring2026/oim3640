## My Project Proposal

## What I'm building:
[For my second mini project, I want to build a tool that assists DJ's when they want to analyze how lyrics of songs from different artists can help them create decks to perform. For example, the DJ will input a multitude of songs from a wide variety of different artists and interpret which songs go well for a deck.]

## Why I chose this:
[As someone who enjoys music and occassional DJing for my personal persuits, this tool would help save so much time for part-time DJs. They would be able to add songs according to the vibe they intend to bring to their deck and can create decks easily. Using an online application like this can solve a time problem.]

## Core features:
## 1
Enables the user to use lyrics from a song source such as Lyrics Genius, Spotify, and/or Apple Music for them to gain the discrography and lists of song to complete the transfer of data.

##2
Showcase an engine that allows for parts of songs to connect using identical lines from different songs, the introduction, and hooks to count how frequent repetitive patterns are.

##3
Comparison between terms that identify the vibe of the music through high and low energy terms. This allows for the syncing of music to be symmetric.

##4
Display different themes of song genres such as nightlife, romanance, house, etc.
